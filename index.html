How to use this app:

This app fetches a list of Pokemon from the official Pokeapi (https://pokeapi.co/).

Each Pokemon is loaded with a list of attributes such as "height", "weight", and the individual type characterization.

Simply click on any of the buttons below or use the Search option above (on mobile, tap on the three bars at the top), to open one of the Pokemon cards. Upon clicking on the respective Pokemon, you can view the information mentioned above.

Close the Pokemon card by clicking the "X" in the top right corner, pressing the "Esc" button on your keyboard, or clicking anywhere outside the Pokemon card with your mouse or finger/pen (on mobile).
